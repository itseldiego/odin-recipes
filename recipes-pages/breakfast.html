Breakfst Burritos

Ingredients:

Base:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 flour tortillas

Fillings:

  • 4 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 4 slices ham, diced
  • 1 cup diced potatoes, cooked

To Serve:

  • Ketchup

Instructions:

Prepare the Ingredients

  1. Cook the bacon in a pan over medium heat until crispy. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, cook the diced ham until lightly browned. Set aside.
  3. In another pan, cook the diced potatoes until golden and crispy. Set aside.

Scramble the Eggs

  1. In a bowl, whisk the eggs with a pinch of salt.
  2. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and cook, stirring occasionally, until fully scrambled.

Assemble the Burritos

  1. Warm the flour tortillas in a pan or microwave for a few seconds.
  2. Evenly distribute the scrambled eggs, bacon, ham, and diced potatoes onto each tortilla.
  3. Drizzle with ketchup, if desired.
  4. Fold the sides of each tortilla in, then roll tightly to form a burrito.

Serve immediately and enjoy!
