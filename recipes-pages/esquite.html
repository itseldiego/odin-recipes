Esquite

Ingredients:

Base:

  • 4 ears of corn
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup crumbled cotija cheese
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Seasonings:

  • ½ tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste

Optional Topping:

  • ¼ cup crushed Hot Cheetos

Instructions:

Prepare the Corn

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
  2. Add the ears of corn and boil for 5-7 minutes until tender.
  3. Remove the corn and let it cool slightly.
  4. Using a knife, carefully cut the kernels off the cob.

Assemble the Esquites

  1. In a pan over medium heat, melt the butter.
  2. Add the corn kernels and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Divide the cooked corn into cups.
  4. Stir in mayonnaise, crumbled cotija cheese, chili powder, and salt to taste.
  5. Top with crushed Hot Cheetos if desired.
  6. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the top before serving.

Serve immediately and enjoy!
