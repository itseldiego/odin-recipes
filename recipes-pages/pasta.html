Fettuccine Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients:

Pasta:

  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup reserved pasta water
Sauce: 2 tbsp butter 2 tbsp flour ½ cup half-and-half ½ cup heavy cream 2 oz cream cheese ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese Spices: ½ tsp paprika ½ tsp ground black pepper Salt to taste ½ tsp onion powder ½ tsp garlic powder
Instructions: Cook the Pasta Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine and cook for 15 minutes or until al dente. Before draining, reserve 1 cup of pasta water and set aside. Make the Sauce In a pan over medium heat, melt 2 tbsp of butter. Add flour and stir constantly until it turns slightly brown. Slowly whisk in ½ cup half-and-half and ½ cup heavy cream, stirring until the mixture thickens. Incorporate Cheese Lower the heat and add cream cheese and Parmesan cheese. Stir gently to allow them to melt slowly for a smooth consistency. Adjust the Sauce Pour in 1 cup of reserved pasta water to loosen the sauce. Add paprika, ground black pepper, salt, onion powder, and garlic powder. Stir well until everything is combined. Combine & Serve Add the cooked fettuccine to the sauce, tossing to coat evenly. Serve immediately and enjoy!
