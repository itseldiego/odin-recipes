Fettuccine Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients:

Pasta:

  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup reserved pasta water

Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • ½ cup half-and-half
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 2 oz cream cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Spices:

  • ½ tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • ½ tsp garlic powder

Instructions:

Cook the Pasta

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add fettuccine and cook for 15 minutes or until al dente.
  3. Before draining, reserve 1 cup of pasta water and set aside.

Make the Sauce

  1. In a pan over medium heat, melt 2 tbsp of butter.
  2. Add flour and stir constantly until it turns slightly brown.
  3. Slowly whisk in ½ cup half-and-half and ½ cup heavy cream, stirring until the mixture thickens.

Incorporate Cheese

  1. Lower the heat and add cream cheese and Parmesan cheese.
  2. Stir gently to allow them to melt slowly for a smooth consistency.
  3. Pour in 1 cup of reserved pasta water to loosen the sauce.
  4. Add paprika, ground black pepper, salt, onion powder, and garlic powder.
  5. Stir well until everything is combined.

Combine & Serve

  1. Add the cooked fettuccine to the sauce, tossing to coat evenly.

Serve immediately and enjoy!
