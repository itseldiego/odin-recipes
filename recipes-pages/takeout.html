Ingredients:

Recipe: Takeout

Ingredients:

  • 1 phone
  • 1 credit card
  • Your favorite restaurant’s menu

Instructions:

  1. Pick up your phone and dial your favorite restaurant.
  2. Expertly read the menu and place your order.
  3. Provide payment details and confirm the delivery or pickup time.
  4. Wait patiently (or pace around the house while hungry).
  5. Receive your food with gratitude and excitement.
  6. Enjoy your meal with zero cooking effort—you're a true culinary mastermind!

Bon appétit! Or in this case... easy peasy!
