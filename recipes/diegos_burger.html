Diego's burger recipe

Don Diego's Best Burger

This burger is a flavorful delight with sauteed onions, tangy pickels, and melty pepper jack cheese Topped with zesty chipotle sauce and served on a warm, buttered toasted bread bun for the perfect crunch. Take a bite and experience a delcious combination of savory and spicy. PS have a nice cold drink ready!

Ingredients:

  • Frozen burger patties
  • Buns
  • butter
  • salt
  • pepper
  • onion
  • pepper jack cheese
  • oil
  • tomato
  • jalapenos

Prep Time: 15-30 mins

How to Cook:

  1. First, thaw patties at room temp for 5 minutes
  2. While patties thaw, chop up onions, tomatoes, jalapenos.*keep seperate.
  3. heat up a pan at low heat, 3tbsp of butter. Once melted, add 3 tbsp of oil. Let it heat.
  4. place patties into air fryer and cook for 12 minutes @ 400 degrees
  5. put your stove on high heat, once blend of oil/butter is golden brown, add onions,salt,pepper.
  6. cook onions till desired texture, I recommend charing them up a bit for best flavor.
  7. Toast buns, add chipotle sauce,cheese, cooked patties, and your fresh sauteed onions.
  8. Finally, Enjoy ! :)
