Tres Leches Coffee

Description

*This coffee cannot be made dairy free as its names states 'three milk'. This savory and delicious coffee gives your sweettooth just what it needs.

Ingredients

  • Evaporated milk
  • Sweeened condensed milk
  • Whole milk
  • Expresso of your choice. Recommend Belle Expressod

Directions

  1. first step is to warm a cup of milk to the point of boiling. In another cup, add 1/2 cup of evaporated milk and add Sweeened milk to your taste of sweetness. Once milk is about to boil, turn off and add cup with evaporated/sweetened milk, slowly stir until dissolved in milk. Then add 3 shots of expresso, and serve yourself a cup. If you would like it cold, just add ice and drink up.
