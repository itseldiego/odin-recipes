Flan de Galletas Ricanela

Ingredients

  • Huevos
  • vanilla
  • condensed milk
  • Galletas Ricanela
  • Milk
  • Evaporated milk
  • Sugar

Prep Time

Around 60 minutes

Directions

  1. First, start of by blending and crusing a back of ricanelas until you get sugar crumb form, then place aside
  2. Start off with 5 eggs, make sure they are at room temp. Put into blender
  3. One can of evaporated milk(16oz),1 can of condensed milk, add both two blender
  4. next, get two cups of whole milk and to blender.
  5. add tablespoons of vanilla extract and add to blender.
  6. Blend, and make sure texture is nice and smooth. Strain it and put back in blender.
  7. add Ricanela crumbs to blender, and blend again
  8. Preheat oven to 420 degrees, and while oven is heating up. work on caramel sauce
  9. on a pan, add 1 cup of sugar and melt till its lightly golden. Make sure not to over cook.
  10. add caramel sauce to baking container and spread all around.Let it cool!!
  11. once caramel is cooled, add flan mixture. Next on a bigger size pan, add 3 cups of water and then place flan container inside that pan.
  12. Throw inside oven and cook for 45-60 minutes. After cooked, let it cool for a few hours and refrigerate. Once chilled, its ready to eat.
  13. Make sure to put flan upside down on a different container, and now its ready to eat.
